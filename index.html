Our Process

How We Get Started

A clear, structured process designed for clarity, consistency, and trust—from first conversation to ongoing support.

1

Initial Consultation

A short conversation to understand your organization, current technology, and challenges. No obligation—this is about alignment and transparency. We determine whether managed IT is a fit and what level of support makes sense.

2

IT Health Check

A baseline review covering devices, patch status, security posture, backups, and access configuration. The goal is a clear starting point—not overwhelm.

3

Findings & Recommendations

Plain-language feedback on what's working, where risks exist, and practical steps to improve stability and security. Actionable steps, not unnecessary complexity.

4

Ongoing Managed IT Support

Continuous monitoring, automated patching, threat detection, and responsive remote support. Predictable, consistent, and aligned with your environment for the long haul.
