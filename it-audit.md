## Why It Matters

Most small organizations don't know what they don't know when it comes to IT. An audit removes the guesswork — giving you an honest assessment of where your technology stands, what's at risk, and what to do about it.

This is especially valuable before something goes wrong, before a compliance conversation, or when taking over management of an existing environment.
